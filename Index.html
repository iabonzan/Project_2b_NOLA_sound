About

Nola Sound is a student-run sound equipment company working
out of Loyola University New Orleans since 2005. With state of
the art equipment, we can provide sound for events of any size
throughout the city. Our stagehands and engineers are well trained
on all aspects of making your event run smoothly. Nola Sound has
grown steadily to become a leader in providing value-centric live
concert services. We strive to bring quality live sound to our
community at a competitive rate.
